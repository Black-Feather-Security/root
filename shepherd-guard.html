Churches Are Trusted Communities — and That Trust Must Be Protected.

Churches manage giving platforms, staff accounts, member information, volunteer access, email systems, livestream tools, and sensitive communications. Attackers often target these environments through phishing, compromised accounts, donation fraud, ransomware, and unauthorized logins.

Shepherd Guard gives church leaders a practical way to understand risk, improve security, and know what to do before a technical issue becomes a ministry crisis.
